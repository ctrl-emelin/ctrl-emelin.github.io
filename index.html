about me

introduction background and school affliations


My name is Emelin Flores. I'm a Dominican-American living in Bayonne, NJ. I'm interested in information design- the philophy of why we design our technology the way we do. My primary career interests are in product management and cybersecurity. I'd love to one day develop a product that can help us secure ourselves without putting our privacy at risk.

I am a junior at Rutgers University-New Brunswick majoring in information technology minoring in critical intelligence. I spend most of my time learning. Whether it is through a course on Udemy, a podcast, an audiobook, competing at hackathons, attending conferences or creating my own projects-I love to know why things are the way they are.

Part-time, I am a web developer for Vintage Vinyl Records. At school (part-time), I am the corporate lead for Women in Information Technology Informatics (WITI) at Rutgers.

I love mentorship and learning about how professionals get to where they are now. I have previously been a part of W.I.N.G.S. W.I.N.G.S is a program created by the Douglass Women's college of RU to connect students and Johnson & Johnson professionals in a mentor-mentee environment. In addition I have been a mentee for Out in tech, and #builtbygirls WAVE.

interests present journey and professional outlook


Currently I am interning at Cisco as a Security Solutions intern specializing in privacy, ecurity and compliance. I am learning how to to help customers understand complex security needs by identifying challenges, risks, and vulnerabilities in technology, people, and processes.

In the fall'19 I will be interning for Rutgers Institute for Secure Communities In collaboration with a government agency, performing legal research with a multi-disciplinary data analytics project to automate procedural standards.

As mentioned before, my current passions lie within product management and cybersecurity. I do not know where the future will take me but I hope to blog it out as I continue. Check out my recent posts here.
