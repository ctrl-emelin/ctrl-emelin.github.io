Welcome to my externship experience with NJ Tech council

I had the opportunity to take on a research project that included vetting through startup companies from the NY Tech Day event and PACT Capital Conference. When I selected companies that fit the description, I reached out to those companies to pitch at the 2019 Venture Conference.


I vetted 218 startup companies and gained information using LinkedIn, CrunchBase, and Seamless.ai. The criteria I looked for was if the company was still active, if the company did not exceed 1 million in funding, and if there was enough interest through social media that the company will continue to run. Other criteria requirements included if the company was within the tri-state area, if there was enough contact information, and if the companyâ€™s website or contact information was valid.


As I researched each company I decided to take notice on a couple trends and compiled a dataset to explain the trends I see. These companies can shed some insight on what kind of companies we could expect in 2019. In addition, these data insights are good for NJ Tech Council to see how many companies we can continue to reach out to or conferences to encourage to grow out technical hub in NJ.
