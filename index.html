about me

introduction background and school affliations

My name is Emelin Flores. I'm a Dominican-American living in Bayonne, NJ. I am a junior at Rutgers University-New Brunswick studying information technology minoring in critical intelligence. Outside of school, I am a web developer for Vintage Vinyl Records, a pro intensive member on Codecademy, and a lover of French culture. I am affliated with Society of Hispanic Engineers, #builtbygirls (currently enrolled in the Wave program), the Douglass Women's college at Rutgers University. My next adventure is an internship with NJ Tech Council starting January 2019. When I am not studying or at work I spend my time partcipating in hackathons, attending professional development conferences, and working on my own personal projects.

interests present journey and professional outlook

Aside from school I work on film projects and I am trying to discover who I am. Soul searching is what you might call it. I have done a lot of things, worked jobs to understand industries, and given myself a lot of opportunities to explore fields that are outside my current major studies. I mentioned that I am a multipotentialite. The video linked is of a ted talk which can help you define what a multipotentialite is but I will explain it in some simple terms. A multipotentialite is someone with many interests and creative pursuits. Another way of saying this is that I have no “one true calling” the way specialists do. This may seem bad for some employers or recruiters who are looking for that "passion" person. However, I do not think this inhibits my ability to specialise but makes me someone a "jack of all trades." I am capable of more than meets the eye.
